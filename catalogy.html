👤 Amakuru y'Abantu

Sistema ikubika amakuru yawe mu buryo bwizewe:

  • Kwinjiza ukoresheje fingerprint cyangwa password
  • Polisi ikabona amakuru yawe byihuse
  • Gushyiraho amakosa y'umuhanda n'ibihano
  • Kwishura amande no gukuraho amakosa
  • Amateka yose akabikwa neza

🚗 Amakuru y'Ibinyabiziga

Kubona amakuru menshi y'ikinyabiziga:

  • Kwinjiza licence plate number
  • Kubona amakuru yose y'ikinyabiziga
  • Ifoto n'amakuru birambuye
  • Amateka y'amakosa n'ibihano
  • Ububasha bw'ikinyabiziga

💾 Database Yizewe

Amakuru yose akabikwa mu buryo bwizewe:

  • Encryption ya data yose
  • Backup ya buri munsi
  • Ububasha bwo kugarura amakuru
  • Gufunga neza amakuru y'ibanga
  • Uburenganzira bwo kubona amakuru
